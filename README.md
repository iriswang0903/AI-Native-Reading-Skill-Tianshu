# 📚 AI-Native Reading Method

**Turn any book into a cross-disciplinary knowledge network.**

One book. Multiple disciplines. Thinkers colliding across centuries. You weave the network.

## What It Does

Traditional reading follows the author from A to Z. This method uses AI to break out of the book's framework — tracing every concept's origins and evolution, mapping it across fields, finding the author's blind spots, and colliding ideas from completely different domains.

It also tells you which parts to read yourself. AI handles filtering and connecting. The experience stays with you.

## How It Works

```
Step 1  Panoramic Scan (10 min)        → Worth my time?
Step 2  Taste Test (20-30 min)         → Read 2-3 best passages, choose your path
Step 3  Concept Jumping / Full Read    → AI companion + cross-disciplinary exploration
```

Every concept explored in four directions:

- **Vertical** — Origins, evolution, where it stands today
- **Horizontal** — Same structure across fields, multiple thinkers compared
- **Critical** — Strongest counterarguments, author's blind spots
- **Generative** — Collide ideas, produce insights neither had alone
